## Key Notes

- Broadly, you will have three types of certificates:

  1. Client Certificates
  2. Server Certificates
  3. Root Certificates

- Certificate (public key) | _.crt, _.pem

  - Examples
    - server.crt
    - server.pem
    - client.crt
    - client.pem

- Private key | _.key, _-key.pem
  - Examples
    - server.key
    - server-key.pem
    - client.key
    - client-key.pem
